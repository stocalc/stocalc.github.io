Ashoka QuantFin Club

We explore quant trading and financial modelling using state-of-the-art ML techniques at SCDLDS, Ashoka University. Our aim is to equip students with the skills and opportunities needed to break into quantitative finance — a selective community focused on developing genuine expertise in quantitative methods, algorithmic trading, and financial modeling.

What We Do

  • Explore quantitative trading strategies, backtesting, and execution
  • Build financial models powered by state-of-the-art ML techniques
  • Run reading groups on seminal papers in quant finance and ML
  • Host workshops on programming, statistics, and market microstructure
  • Organize interactions with practitioners from leading quant firms

Our Mission

  • Equip students with the tools to break into quantitative finance
  • Cultivate a selective community of serious quant enthusiasts
  • Bridge rigorous academic theory with real industry practice
  • Develop genuine expertise in algorithmic trading and modeling
  • Create pathways to research, internships, and careers in the field

Focus Areas

  • Statistical arbitrage, market-making, and execution algorithms
  • Derivatives pricing and stochastic volatility modeling
  • Machine learning for financial time series and alpha research
  • Portfolio optimization and risk management
  • Reinforcement learning in trading and market dynamics

Stochastic Calculus & Mathematical Finance

This is a course for advanced undergraduate and graduate students with an aptitude and interest in quantitative methods. The students will be exposed to no-arbitrage-based deep and beautiful ideas underlying mathematical finance. For this they will get an adequate exposure to stochastic calculus. Stochastic calculus is also relevant for the increasingly mainstream diffusion-based generative AI.

Course Period: Monsoon 2025.

Schedule: Tuesday & Thursday, 10:10 – 11:40 AM

Mode: Hybrid (In-person + Online)

Duration: 40 hours of rigorous lectures + student-led projects

Course Content

  • Review of Measure-Theoretic Probability
  • Martingales & Brownian Motion
  • Stochastic Calculus & Stochastic Integration
  • Itô's Formula & Girsanov's Theorem
  • Stochastic Differential Equations & PDE Connections
  • Discrete & Continuous-Time Pricing Theory
  • Stochastic Volatility Models & Interest Rate Models
  • PDEs in Finance

Eligibility

  • Advanced undergraduates & master's-level students
  • Strong background in Probability, Linear Algebra, and Analysis
  • Aptitude & interest in quantitative methods
